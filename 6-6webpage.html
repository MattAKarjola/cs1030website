Terraria Calamity Mod

Terraria is a great sandbox game full of fights and exploration. The comminity surrounding the game have made many mods to enhance the gameplay experience. One of these mods is a large content mod known as Calamity Mod.

Bosses

Calamity mod adds several bosses that terraria does not have by default, these are some of the main bosses for each stage of the game.

  1. Pre-Hardmode: The Slime God
  2. Hardmode: Astrum Aureus
  3. Post-Moon Lord: Supreme Witch, Calamitas
  4. Mini Boss: Eidolon Wyrm

Infernum

Terraria calamity mod has another mod that can be added to enhance the experience even further called Infernum

The main difference between infernum and default calamity mod are boss health and boss damage.

Boss Name Calamity Health Infernum Health
The Slime God 15,920 18,203
Astrum Aureus 100,000 144,074
Supreme Witch, Calamitas 960,000 4,346,342
Eidolon Wyrm 160,000 (unknown on the wiki)
